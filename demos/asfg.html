Our purpose

To elevate the client experience — by first elevating one another.

We provide professional and personal support, information sharing, education, and friendship — to help elevate the client experience and the expertise at every member's business. ASFG itself keeps no clients and gives no advice; its members do, each through their own firm.

Whom our members serve

  • Closely-held businesses
  • Executives
  • Athletes
  • Entertainers
  • Artists
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Retirement plans
  • Endowments

The compact

What membership asks, and what it gives back.

Peer-selected

Admitted by those who know

There is no buying your way in. Every member is nominated and vetted by the advisors already in the room — a standard higher than any brochure.

Fiduciary, always

One standard, no exceptions

Membership is open only to fiduciary advisors. It is the single non-negotiable — the reason a place at this table means something.

Give to the group

Everyone contributes so all benefit

Members give their time and expertise, meeting twice a year and sharing through a private network — so that each advisor feels connected, welcome, and supported.
