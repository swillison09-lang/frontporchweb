Why “Front Porch”?

Websites Built on Trust

Hi, I am Sean, a high school junior studying aviation and someone who loves learning about flight and technology. Being part of an adoptive family, I have heard about how much time and effort my mom put into building a website as we were waiting to grow our family. Hearing this story, along with my own love of technology, inspired me to create a platform where families and small organizations can more easily and economically share stories online.

I named it Front Porch because a front porch is where neighbors stop by, stories get shared, and trust is built one honest conversation at a time. That’s how I want to work with you. You’ll see the site before it goes live, you’ll know the price before you commit, and you can always just ask me a question and get a straight answer. Whether you run a local business, are growing your family, or are a student chasing a goal, I’d be honored to help you tell your story well.

— Sean, Founder
