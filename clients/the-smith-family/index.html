In our own words

A letter to you

We hope this letter finds you well. We are the Smith family, and we want to begin by expressing our gratitude for the opportunity to write to you and share a little about ourselves.

We understand this is a meaningful and deeply important decision, and we want to acknowledge the care and love that has gone into considering what is best for your child. We want you to know that we already hold a great deal of respect for you.

A little about us: we are a loving, supportive family who values kindness, stability, and spending quality time together. We enjoy doing everyday things as a family—sharing meals, going outdoors, and creating a home filled with laughter and encouragement. We are excited about the possibility of welcoming a child into our lives and giving them a safe, caring environment where they can grow and thrive.

If we are chosen, we promise to provide a home filled with love, support, and opportunity. We will encourage their interests, support their education, and make sure they always know how deeply they are loved. We also believe it is important for children to understand where they come from, and we will always speak about you with respect and gratitude.

We cannot fully understand everything you are feeling right now, but we do want you to know that we admire your strength. Please know that your child will always be cared for and valued.

Thank you again for taking the time to read our letter. We are honored to be considered and hope you feel peace and confidence in your journey.

With respect and gratitude,
The Smith Family
